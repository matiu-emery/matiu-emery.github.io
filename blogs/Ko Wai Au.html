About Me

who am I?

Kia ora koutou, ko Matiu taku ingoa. I live in Kirikiriroa (Hamilton) with my little whānau. My nickname is Much and I grew up in Onehunga, a suburb of Tāmaki (Auckland) with my mum, 5 siblings and grandfather.

On my mums side im from Muaūpoko, Ngāti Kahungunu ki Wairarapa, Te Whakatōhea, Ngāi Tai, Ngāi Tūhoe & Ngāti Apakura. On my dads side im from Waikato, Ngāti Maniapoto & Ngāti Apakura.

People would say that I am polite, caring and very talkative. My key values would be whanaungatanga (kinship), kaitiakitanga (guardianship) and maanakitanga( being supportive).

Throughout my adult life, I worked as a Glazier and Truck Driver. However, in 2021, I suffered an injury at work that required surgery. The following year, another injury led to a meeting with a specialist, who advised me that I needed a career change as my back couldn't handle the physical demands of my job. As I considered my options for a new challenge, my partner suggested I look into Dev Academy. I decided to pursue it because I enjoy computers and the technical side of things to get a better understanding on how things work. I want to improve my skills and it will benefit the future of not only myself but also my whānau. Nga mihi
